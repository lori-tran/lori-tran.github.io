Van Leeuwen

A quick ad with clips exploring framing, movement, color, and product storytelling. Shot and edited to feel sweet, playful, and delicious.

I created this ad to capture Van Leeuwen's essence of simplicity, quality, and minimalism. Their brand philosophy centers around clean aesthetics and premium ingredients, so I focused on simple colors and movements that let the product speak for itself. The editing emphasizes quality over flashy effects, maintaining the brand's sophisticated yet approachable personality.
